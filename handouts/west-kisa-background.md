# West (K7G2-87459-WST) — Character Background
**Played by Kisa · Cradle of Embers (Stars Without Number)**
*As submitted by the player. This is canon; do not paraphrase away the specifics.*

---

## Background

K7G2-87459-WST — "**West**" — is a clone. Eighteen months old, pretrained with all the skills needed to perform their function.

They are **Grade 2 Security, Kael-7 lineage**, produced by a biotech cartel called the **Progenitor Combine**, which manufactures people the way a defense contractor manufactures weapons. There are a lot of Kael-7s out in the sector — West is one of thousands of the same lineage, same training, same design. Standard issue security clone.

Their obedience is not software. It is genetic. They are physiologically compelled to follow orders from a legitimate authority, almost instinctually. Countermanding them feels physically wrong, like trying to hold your breath. They were made to protect people. They are good at it.

They are part of a **three-unit security detail**, all Kael-7 Grade 2, all equals. The unit was purchased together and deployed together. There is no lead clone. They work as a unit.

## About Synthesis Prime

The Progenitor Combine's founding world, built around cloning vaults that go deeper than anyone will tell you. West spent their first twelve or thirteen months there in conditioning. White floors. Calibrated acoustics. Instructors who were professional and consistent. The Combine is good at what it does, and Synthesis Prime is where it does it. West left when the unit was sold to the Hollow Covenant and has not been back. It's in the Lyreth System, hex H03.

## About the Hollow Covenant

The faction that purchased the unit — a religious group split down the middle into two factions that barely tolerate each other.

- **The Remembrance** wants to restore a dead empire; they believe the old ways were correct and the collapse was a betrayal. Military leader: **Marshal Tran**, who has been getting increasingly reckless in ways that worry those around him.
- **The Becoming** thinks restoration is a dead end; they want to become something the old world never had the courage to be. Leader: **Elder Vesper** — warm, direct, moves like someone who has decided her body is an instrument. She laughs easily and means it; when she's serious the warmth doesn't leave, it sharpens. One of the few people in this story who might actually be trustworthy.

**The unit's chain of command runs through Elder Vesper.**

## About Arbiter Senn

Hollow Covenant diplomat and the unit's legal owner. She was attending the Thessavar Concordance at the private island estate called **The Still Gardens**, negotiating an excavation treaty that would give the Covenant access to alien ruins under Thessavar's ocean — the treaty a lot of powerful people wanted dead or delayed. She has not acknowledged the security unit's existence since they boarded her boat. The clones have been on threat assessment the whole time and have flagged eleven people as worth monitoring.

## About Requisition Agent Tarek

The Combine agent who delivered the unit to the Covenant. Broad-shouldered, always dressed right for the room, warm in a way that feels practiced but is probably real. Before he disappeared he left a sealed message addressed to the unit: *"If you are reading this, something went wrong. Trust Elder Vesper. Do not trust the relic."* Nobody knows what he found, or whether he's still alive.
